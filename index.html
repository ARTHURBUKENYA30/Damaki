Our Safari Experiences

Explore thrilling wildlife tours, majestic landscapes, and authentic cultural engagements. We tailor your adventures to match your spirit of discovery.

Gorilla Trekking in Bwindi and Mgahinga

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime journey to meet the gentle giants of the forest — mountain gorillas. Guided by experienced trackers, witness these incredible creatures in their natural habitat and create memories that last forever. This intimate encounter offers a profound connection to nature and an unforgettable wildlife experience.

Chimpanzee Tracking in Kibale Forest

Discover Uganda’s rich primate diversity with a thrilling chimpanzee trek through the enchanting Kibale Forest National Park. Watch our closest relatives in their playful and social behaviors as they swing through the canopy and interact in fascinating ways. The trek offers incredible birdwatching opportunities as well.

Classic Wildlife Safaris in Queen Elizabeth & Murchison Falls

Experience the drama of the African savannah. Spot elephants, lions, buffalo, hippos, and countless bird species across diverse landscapes. Enjoy exciting game drives, serene boat cruises along the Kazinga Channel, and breathtaking views of the powerful Murchison Falls. The park’s diverse ecosystems promise remarkable sightings around every corner.
